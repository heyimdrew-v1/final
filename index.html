Audio and Video Production Technology

by Drew Swanson

Program Description

Audio and Video Production technology will develop professional skills in digital audio and video production, including editing, mixing, and mastering. Training will focus in speech, writing, production planning, postproduction and distribution. You will also be studying the history of media and will get hands-on training in production. Training will include troubleshooting, collaboration, management of time, planning, and other skills that are needed to succeed in the industry. Once a student completes this degree, they will be able to produce audio and video content for films, television, and other media employement opportunities.

Job Opportunities

Job opportunities for audio and video production are very common and needed today, with technology advancing and the use of good audio quality and video quality in high demand, a lot of jobs are offered to those with degree's or with experience. Graduates of the Audio and Video Production Technology program can pursue various careers such as:

  • Sound Engineer
  • Video Editor
  • Production Manager
  • Audio/Video Technicians
  • Audio Visual Technician
  • Video Technician
  • Video Producer
  • Field Technician (Audio, Video Installation)
